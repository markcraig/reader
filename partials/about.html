Reader

This site uses the Google Feed API. It sets a cookie to avoid displaying items that you have already seen. But you can click the Reload button to delete the cookie and reload all feeds.

This page stores the list of your feeds in your browser's local storage, so before you change browsers, download your list of feeds. You can then upload the list in the other browser.

The source code for this site is available at Github.com.

This site also relies on AngularJS, and on Bootstrap.
